## 2/8 — The Problem + Onboarding

Government data lags 10 days *at best*. Sometimes a full month. A LOT can change in 30 days when you're deciding whether to sell canola at $14 or hold for $15.50.

**Bushel Board's play:** combine real-time farmer activity data with lagging government releases so you can see the market moving BEFORE the official numbers confirm it.

Setup takes about 60 seconds. Enter your acres grown per crop — that's it.

Why acres? Three reasons:
→ Your dashboard only shows crops YOU grow. Less noise.
→ It helps model next-year production to inform forward selling.
→ It filters out non-farmers. If someone claims 300 bu/ac canola, the math won't check out. Keeps the data clean for everyone.

Starting with crop year 2025-2026.

## 3/8 — Your Farm Profile

Once you're in, you get a private crop card showing your position on every grain you grow. **Nobody sees this but you.**

Hit "Log Delivery" whenever you haul — enter the tonnes, optionally tag the elevator. That delivery data does two things:

1. **Tracks your position** — you always know exactly where you stand.
2. **Ranks you against the network** — Bushel Board calculates what percentile of producers have delivered that grain. If 80% of farmers still have their wheat in the bin and you've already moved half... that's a signal.

This is real-time producer sentiment you literally cannot get anywhere else. Not from the CGC. Not from your grain buyer. From farmers, for farmers.

---

## 4/8 — AI-Powered Weekly Summary

Every week when new CGC data drops, you get a personalized **Farm Summary** powered by AI.

It cross-references YOUR deliveries against the national market data, scans agricultural news and X for the current narrative, and distills it into one actionable briefing.

Think of it as a market analyst that works for YOU instead of a grain company.

The AI gets smarter over time. You can rate each summary — on point or off the mark — and that feedback trains your personal model to deliver sharper insights every week.

This is one of the bigger costs to run, so I'm actively optimizing models and may swap engines as better options emerge. The goal is maximum insight per dollar.

## 5/8 — The Overview Dashboard

The Overview tab is your command center.

**Quick-look cards** for every grain on your farm — market direction, weekly movement, and key signals at a glance. These cards get richer as more data sources come online.

**Community Sentiment Bar** — see how farmers across Bushel Board are feeling about each market. Are producers holding or hauling? Real-time, anonymized, powerful.

**X Intelligence Feed** — we scan farm-related X posts multiple times daily and score them as bearish, neutral, or bullish. You see the source, the grain, and the sentiment. The AI filter improves over time as it learns what's actually relevant vs. noise.

**Market Pulse** — a concise AI summary tying everything together.

---

## 6/8 — Grain Detail Pages

Click into any grain and you get the full picture.

**Interactive data dashboards** — deliveries, exports, receipts, processing — all visualized so you can see trends without reading spreadsheets.

**Active Thesis** — the AI builds and refines a market thesis for each grain every week. It starts simple and gets sharper as more data flows in. Think of it as a living research note.

**Market Signals from Data** — key growth points and pain points extracted from the numbers.

**Market Signals from X** — bullish/bearish sentiment scored for that specific grain. You can vote on whether a signal is relevant or dismiss it. That engagement makes the feed smarter for everyone.

The more farmers participate, the better this gets for ALL farmers. Network effect working FOR the producer for once.

---

## 7/8 — Farmer Sentiment Poll

Below the data on each grain page: **Hold or Haul?**

After you've reviewed the market data, the thesis, and the signals — cast your weekly vote. Are you holding this grain or moving it?

This creates a real-time sentiment index of what producers are ACTUALLY thinking after looking at the data. Not what an analyst in Winnipeg thinks. What farmers on the ground think.

The sentiment resets weekly, but every week's data is saved. Over time we'll be able to track how farmer sentiment correlated with actual deliveries (with that 10-day government lag). That's a leading indicator no one else has.

## 8/8 — The Supply Pipeline Map

The final piece: a live map showing grain flow across the prairies.

**Total deliveries by province.** How much is sitting at elevators, ports, processors, and still in farmer bins. The full pipeline in one view.

**Pipeline Velocity** layers your personal deliveries onto the national flow so you can see exactly how your marketing decisions line up with the broader market.

This is the kind of visibility that grain companies pay millions to build internally. We're building it for farmers.
